Evan Diamente

I am a marketing professional with more than three years of experience in the real estate industry, currently working at Edward Rose & Sons, where I oversee portfolio-level marketing strategy across 35 Class A through C multifamily assets. This portfolio spans new development and construction, lease-up communities, redevelopments, and stabilized same-store properties, encompassing more than 10,000 units within the firm’s broader 70,000-unit owner-operator enterprise. My work centers on aligning digital performance, brand positioning, and revenue objectives within a large-scale operational framework, ensuring that marketing functions as an integrated driver of occupancy and asset performance. With a foundation in single-family housing and a management degree from Indiana University, I approach marketing as an operating discipline grounded in creativity, systems thinking, and measurable impact.

About Me

Beyond
the work.

I grew up with a curiosity for how spaces and communities are built, which naturally led me into real estate marketing. At Edward Rose & Sons, I focus on connecting people to places through thoughtful storytelling and strategy — but outside of work, I'm drawn to quiet, intentional pursuits that help me slow down and observe. I enjoy exploring the rhythm of a city and finding unexpected beauty in architecture, design, and the way people move through a space.

Grounding this curiosity has been the sport of swimming. Competing at an international level has demanded discipline, focus, and resilience — qualities that mirror the way I approach my work and the world around me, shaping both the projects I take on and the life I choose to lead.
